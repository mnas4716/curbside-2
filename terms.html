The consult flow

From a question to a documented opinion — in one session.

No phone tag, no waiting on a written referral. Three steps, while the patient is still with you.

01

Write the case once

Enter the patient, the question, and attach photos or results. Curbside structures it into a clean specialist brief and flags what's missing — before anyone joins.

02

Reach an on-call specialist

The case broadcasts to verified specialists in that field who are available now. The first to accept opens a secure video room with you in seconds.

03

Walk away documented

The live scribe captures the discussion. On completion you get a SOAP note, a letter to the GP, a referral and a patient handout — plus the correct MBS items.

About Curbside

A "curbside consult", made real.

Clinicians have always relied on the informal "curbside" — catching a colleague in the corridor for a quick, expert second opinion. It's fast and it's invaluable. It's also undocumented, unbillable, and impossible when the specialist isn't down the hall.

Curbside is that conversation, rebuilt for telehealth: structured, secure, properly billed, and written up automatically. We're a clinician-founded Australian health-technology company focused on closing the distance between primary and specialist care.

Questions, answered

Frequently asked

Is the patient present during the consult?+
Yes. Curbside is designed around the patient being in the room with their GP for the whole consult, with the specialist joining by video. That's what makes the GP's Patient End Support (PES) item claimable and keeps the patient at the centre of the decision.
How does billing work?+
When a consult completes, Curbside recommends the correct MBS items: the GP's PES item (2484–2495, chosen by face-to-face time and setting) and the specialist's video attendance item (91822 initial / 91823 subsequent). You review and confirm each item before anything is submitted. Schedule fees shown are indicative and should be validated against MBS Online — Curbside is billing support, not billing advice.
Who can join the network?+
Registered Australian GPs and specialists. Every account is verified against AHPRA by an administrator before it can create, accept or bill a consult. Until then, accounts remain read-only.
What about privacy and patient data?+
Video runs on encrypted, single-use rooms that expire after the consult. Clinical records, notes and the audit trail are retained as part of the medical record; we don't sell data or use it for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for the detail.
Do specialists need to be available all the time?+
No. Specialists toggle availability on and off, or set scheduled windows. You only ever see — and only ever receive — consults while you've chosen to be available.
Does it replace a formal referral?+
It complements it. Many questions resolve on the call. When ongoing specialist care is needed, Curbside drafts the formal referral letter for you, so the onward pathway is faster too.
